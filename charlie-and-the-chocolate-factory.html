Description

Sir Willy Wonka is the strangest, most bizarre and most extraordinary chocolate maker in the world!

Charlie Bucket's amazing adventure begins when he finds a precious Golden Ticket belonging to Sir Willy Wonka and wins a full day of fun in the strange chocolate factory. Little did he know that there were many surprises waiting for him ahead!

Roald Dahl (September 13, 1916 – November 23, 1990)

Roald Dahl was a spy, a yoke pilot, a chocolate historian, and a medical inventor. He is also the author of Charlie and the Chocolate Factory, Matilda Girl, BFG – The Friendly Giant… and many other great stories. Roald Dahl was named the World's No. 1 Storyteller.

Roald Dahl's books are published in more than 55 languages ​​worldwide, with tens of millions of copies in circulation. Many of his books top lists of the best children's literature of all time.
