Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets

Paperback

J. K. Rowling

200.000 ₫


Description

Harry agonized over the end of his horrible summer vacation with the Dursleys. But a poor little house-elf warned Harry of the deadly danger that awaited him at Hogwarts.

Back at school, Harry hears a rumor circulating about the chamber of secrets, which holds terrifying mysteries for wizards of Muggle origin. Someone is casting a spell that paralyzes people, leaving them almost dead, and a horrifying warning is found on the wall. The prime suspect - and always wrong - is Harry. But something even darker than that was revealed.
