Flow

Paperback

Mihaly Csikszentmihalyi

155.040 ₫


Description

For those who want to optimize their ability to focus to find coherence at work, towards fulfillment, and happiness in all life experiences.

Have you ever been so completely absorbed in a good book, job, or performance that you didn't notice hours had passed, you didn't even have an idea of ​​what was going on around you? own existence? At that moment, a strange pleasure flows through you, to the point that you are constantly looking for the same feeling again, but it seems impossible, because you do not really understand the feeling. what it is, as well as the origin of that wonderful experience.

The answer will be found in " Flow" a book that summarizes the lifetime work of Hungarian-American psychologist Mihaly Csikszentmihalyi - the great tree of contemporary psychology. Flow is a harmoniously ordered state of consciousness, " in which one engages in an activity so profoundly that nothing else seems to matter ", one will resolve to perform an activity solely for the sake of doing it.
